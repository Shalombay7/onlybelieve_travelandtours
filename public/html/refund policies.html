Refund and Cancellation Policy

Introduction

Only Believe Travel and Tour is committed to providing quality travel services with transparency and fairness. This policy outlines the terms and conditions for cancellations and refunds to ensure clarity and mutual understanding between our clients and the company.

1. Cancellation by the Client

Clients may cancel their bookings under the following conditions:

A. Flight Bookings:

Cancellations are subject to airline policies and fare rules. Refund eligibility depends on the airline’s cancellation terms. Some tickets may be non-refundable or subject to penalties. Processing fees may apply.

B. Hotel Reservations:

Refunds depend on the hotel’s cancellation policy. Some hotels allow free cancellations within a specified period, while others charge a penalty. No-shows may result in full charges with no refund.

C. Tour Packages & Vacation Packages:

Cancellations made 30 days or more before departure: 80% refund (excluding service fees).
Cancellations made 15-29 days before departure: 50% refund.
Cancellations made less than 15 days before departure: No refund.

D. Visa Assistance Services:

Visa fees and service charges are non-refundable once processing has begun, regardless of approval or rejection. For work visa packages, cancellation and refunds will take between 90 to 120 days. Costs incurred for job offers and permits, including other charges, will apply. Applicants are assured of between 50% to 70% refund of the total cost involved.

E. Car Rentals & Transfers:

Free cancellation up to 48 hours before the scheduled pickup. Cancellations made less than 48 hours before pickup may result in partial or full charges.

2. Cancellation by Only Believe Travel and Tour

The company reserves the right to cancel services under the following circumstances:

Unforeseen circumstances such as natural disasters, political instability, or airline/hotel shutdowns. In such cases, clients will receive a full refund or an option to reschedule at no extra cost.

3. Refund Processing

Refund requests must be made in writing via email or through our customer service channels. Refunds will be processed within 7-21 business days, depending on the service provider’s policies, except for work permit visas, which take 90 to 120 days. All refunds will be made using the original payment method.

4. Modifications & Rescheduling

Clients may request changes to their bookings, subject to availability and applicable fees. Date changes for flights, hotels, or tours may incur additional charges.

5. Non-Refundable Services

The following services are non-refundable once booked:

  • Visa processing fees
  • Non-refundable flight tickets
  • Fully prepaid hotel stays with no cancellation option
  • Travel insurance fees
